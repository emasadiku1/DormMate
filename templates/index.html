Why this exists

Three group chats. Zero agreement.

Someone fronts the electric bill and forgets to ask for it back. Someone's skipped the dishes for two weeks. Nobody remembers whose turn it is to buy paper towels. None of that is a person problem — it's a tracking problem, and right now the only place it's tracked is in someone's head.

One app, three jobs

Money, chores, and the list — finally in the same place.

MONEY

Split it, settle it, forget the math.

Log an expense, split it equal, by percentage, or custom, and DormMate works out who owes who — down to the fewest payments needed to zero everyone out.

CHORES

A rotation nobody has to police.

Assign chores to a person or let them auto-rotate through the household. Overdue tasks surface on their own — no one has to be the one who brings it up.

THE LIST

One shopping list, not four texts.

Add what's needed, check it off when it's bought, and link the purchase straight to an expense so the list and the ledger never disagree.
